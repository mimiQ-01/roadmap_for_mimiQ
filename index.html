Introduction

This site has everything you need before contributing to mimiQ++, hell even if you don't contribute, you can learn C/C++ programming concepts, and fundamental quantum computing concepts as well! This site is designed for the absolute beginners. The ONLY prerequisite needed is your time and effort.

If you are already strong in fundamentals of C++ and quantum computing, you may directly jump to mimiQ++ v1.0 source code explanation journey that explains all the code in meticulous detail. Click here to go there. The site follows what I call the ZENyuzien's ink - All content on the sites including the future ones will be ensured that the content is understandable with minimal effort. This however only applies when followed the content in order. When skipped content, its on you. So It is strictly advised to NOT skip anything unless you're really sure. So here is how it goes - You gotta finish two tracks - the quantum track, and the programming track. You will have separate links for each to direct you to the appropriate page as you scroll down.
